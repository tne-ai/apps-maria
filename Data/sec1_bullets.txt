The Gross Regional Product (GRP) for San Diego County from 2019 to 2023 shows a consistent upward trend, indicating economic growth over the years. Here's a detailed analysis:

1. **2019**:
   - Total GRP: $244.28 billion
   - Population: 3,330,458
   - GRP per Capita: $73,346.92

2. **2020**:
   - Total GRP: $244.82 billion
   - Population: 3,296,045
   - GRP per Capita: $74,277.60
   - Analysis: Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, San Diego County's GRP saw a slight increase. The GRP per capita also increased, which can be attributed to a decrease in population.

3. **2021**:
   - Total GRP: $268.87 billion
   - Population: 3,274,954
   - GRP per Capita: $82,099.95
   - Analysis: The economy rebounded significantly in 2021, with a notable increase in GRP and GRP per capita. This reflects a recovery from the pandemic's economic impact.

4. **2022**:
   - Total GRP: $296.68 billion
   - Population: 3,276,208
   - GRP per Capita: $90,557.10
   - Analysis: The growth trend continued in 2022, with both total GRP and GRP per capita showing substantial increases. This suggests strong economic performance and possibly increased productivity or higher value-added activities.

5. **2023**:
   - Total GRP: $308.71 billion
   - Population: 3,252,468
   - GRP per Capita: $94,915.87
   - Analysis: The GRP reached its highest point in 2023, with a continued increase in GRP per capita. The slight decrease in population alongside the increase in GRP suggests improved economic conditions and possibly higher efficiency or investment in high-value sectors.

**Overall Analysis**:
- San Diego County's economy has shown resilience and growth from 2019 to 2023, with a consistent increase in both total GRP and GRP per capita.
- The economic recovery post-2020 pandemic is evident, with significant growth in 2021 and beyond.
- The increase in GRP per capita over the years indicates that the economic growth has outpaced population growth, suggesting improvements in productivity or shifts towards higher-value economic activities.
