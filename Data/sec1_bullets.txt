### San Diego County GRP Analysis (2019-2023)

#### Overview
The Gross Regional Product (GRP) is a measure of the economic output of a region. It is analogous to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) at the national level. The GRP data for San Diego County provides insights into the economic performance of the region over the years.

#### GRP Trends
1. **Total GRP (in dollars):**
   - 2019: $244.28 billion
   - 2020: $244.82 billion
   - 2021: $268.87 billion
   - 2022: $296.68 billion
   - 2023: $308.71 billion

2. **Population:**
   - 2019: 3,330,458
   - 2020: 3,296,045
   - 2021: 3,274,954
   - 2022: 3,276,208
   - 2023: 3,252,468

3. **GRP Per Capita:**
   - 2019: $73,346.92
   - 2020: $74,277.60
   - 2021: $82,099.95
   - 2022: $90,557.10
   - 2023: $94,915.87

#### Analysis
- **Growth in GRP:** San Diego County's GRP has shown a consistent increase from 2019 to 2023, with a notable rise in 2021 and 2022. The total GRP increased by approximately 26.4% from 2019 to 2023.
  
- **Population Trends:** The population of San Diego County has slightly decreased over the years, which is a factor to consider when analyzing per capita figures.

- **GRP Per Capita Growth:** The GRP per capita has increased significantly, indicating that the economic output per person has improved. This suggests increased productivity or higher value economic activities in the region.

San Diego County has demonstrated robust economic growth from 2019 to 2023, with significant increases in both total GRP and GRP per capita.
