### Key Observations

1. **Growth in Total GRP**: San Diego County's total GRP has shown a consistent increase from 2019 to 2023. The GRP grew from approximately $244.28 billion in 2019 to $308.71 billion in 2023. This represents an overall increase of about 26.4% over the five-year period.

2. **Per Capita GRP Growth**: The GRP per capita also increased significantly over the same period. It rose from $73,346.92 in 2019 to $94,915.87 in 2023, marking an increase of approximately 29.4%. This indicates that the economic output per person in San Diego County has been growing, suggesting improvements in productivity or economic conditions.

3. **Yearly Trends**: 
   - The year 2020 saw a slight increase in total GRP despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, indicating resilience in the county's economy.
   - The most significant growth in both total GRP and per capita GRP occurred between 2021 and 2022, with a notable increase in economic activity.

4. **Comparison with State and National Levels**: While the data provided focuses on San Diego County, it is important to compare these trends with state and national data to understand the county's economic performance relative to broader trends.

San Diego County has experienced robust economic growth from 2019 to 2023, both in terms of total GRP and GRP per capita.
