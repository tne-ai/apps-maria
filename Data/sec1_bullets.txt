### Key Observations

1. **Growth in Total GRP**:

2. **Growth in GRP Per Capita**:
   - The GRP per capita also showed a significant increase from $73,346.92 in 2019 to $94,915.87 in 2023, marking a growth of approximately 29.4%.

3. **Yearly Trends**:

   - The most substantial growth occurred between 2020 and 2021, with a notable increase in both total GRP and GRP per capita, indicating a strong economic recovery and expansion during this period.
   - The growth trend continued through 2022 and 2023, albeit at a slightly slower pace compared to the 2020-2021 period.

San Diego County's economy has demonstrated robust growth over the past five years, with significant increases in both total GRP and GRP per capita.
